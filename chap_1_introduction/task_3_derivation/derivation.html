3. 其他导数

使用右乘\(\mathfrak{so}(3)\)，推导以下导数：

\[ \rm \frac{d(R^{-1}p)}{dR} \tag{19} \] \[ \rm \frac{dln(R_1 R_2^{-1})}{dR_2} \tag{20} \]

3.1.

设\(\rm R\)对应的李代数为\(\rm \phi\)

\[ \rm R = exp(\phi^ \wedge) \] \[ \rm R^T = exp(-\phi^ \wedge) \]

设\(\rm \Delta R\)对应的李代数为\(\rm \varphi\),则：

\[ \rm \frac{d(R^{-1}p)}{dR} = \lim_{\varphi \rightarrow 0} \frac{exp(-\phi^ \wedge)exp(\varphi^ \wedge)p - exp(-\phi^ \wedge)p}{\varphi} \] \[ \rm = \lim_{\varphi \rightarrow 0} \frac{exp(-\phi^ \wedge)\left(exp(\varphi^ \wedge) - I\right)p}{\varphi} \] \[ \rm \because exp(\varphi^ \wedge) = I + \varphi^ \wedge + O(\varphi^ \wedge) \] \[ \rm \therefore \frac{d(R^{-1}p)}{dR} = \lim_{\varphi \rightarrow 0} \frac{exp(-\phi^ \wedge)\varphi^ \wedge p}{\varphi} = \lim_{\varphi \rightarrow 0} \frac{-exp(-\phi^ \wedge)p^ \wedge \varphi}{\varphi} \] \[ \rm \therefore \frac{d(R^{-1}p)}{dR} = -Rp^ \wedge \]

3.2.

根据第一节课程资料中，公式(16)已知：

\[ \rm \frac{dln(R_1 R_2)}{dR_2} = J_r^{-1}\left(ln(R_1 R_2) ^ \vee\right) \tag{16} \]

记\(R_3 = R_1 R_2^{-1} R_2^{-1}\)

\[ \rm \because R_1 R_2^{-1} = (R_1 R_2^{-1} R_2^{-1}) R_2 = R_3 R_2 \] \[ \rm \frac{dln(R_1 R_2^{-1})}{dR_2} = \frac{dln(R_3 R_2)}{dR_2} = J_r^{-1}\left(ln(R_3 R_2) ^ \vee\right) \] \[ \rm \therefore \frac{dln(R_1 R_2^{-1})}{dR_2} = J_r^{-1}\left(ln(R_1 R_2^{-1}) ^ \vee\right) \]
